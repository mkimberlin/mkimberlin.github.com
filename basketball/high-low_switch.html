Wolves 1-2-2

High-low switch
1) Big guys start high and screen down to open up the guards for an entry pass to either side.

2) The ball-side post screens high.

3) The point cuts to the lane. Obviously, if this creates a look for the point, we want to take it.

4) Otherwise, we rotate the ball back up top.

5) The cutter screens out, while the opposite wing screens down.  This creates a cut opportunity on the side the ball game from and a potential jumper on the other side, depending on how defense reacts.  The ball can swing either direction from here.

6) Again the high screen from the ball-side post to create a lane opportunity.
