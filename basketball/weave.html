Wolves 1-3-1

Weave
1) Point guard passes to the wing and then cuts past a screen by the high post on the strong side.

2) Point guard hands the ball off to the guard on the wing while setting a screen on his man.

The guard on the wing takes the handoff and drive to the lane.

The high post shifts to the weak side to clear room and act as a safety.

The low post screens the weak side wing, who cuts baseline in hope of an open look.

3) Layup off of the drive or use the pass option across to the weak side.

4) For the baseline jumper.
