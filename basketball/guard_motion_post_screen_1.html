Offense 1-3-1

Guard Motion - Post Screen - 1
1) When the point guard either wishes to change things up, or is having difficulty getting off an initial pass to the wing, he will shout "Rotate!" and indicate a direction (clockwise or counter-clockwise).

The guards then rotate in that direction, with the point guard dribbling in the direction of the rotation.  As the wing crosses under the basket, the high post drops down to set a screen for the low post.

2) The low post makes a diagonal cut to the elbow and, if the opportunity presents itself, takes the pass and shot.

3) After setting the screen, the high post rolls off and across to the strong side of the lane for a post up.

4) Ideally, this motion should have allowed one or both of the post players to free up on the strong side for the shot.

5) Score!
