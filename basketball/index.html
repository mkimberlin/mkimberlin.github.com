Parents, Guardians and Spectators Code of Conduct

Rockwood Community Education works to provide valuable lifelong learning experience for patrons of all ages. Part of the experience includes ensuring a safe and enjoyable environment for participants and spectators of programs. As a result, the following code of conduct has been created for all Rockwood Community Education recreation activities.

Parents, Guardians and Spectators shall:

  • Remain in the spectator areas during all games
  • Respect the integrity of the officials/game committee/field supervisor and not advise them how to call the game
  • Model sportsmanship for children by treating all coaches, officials, supervisors and players/parents of either team with courtesy and respect
  • Applaud good and fair play during games
  • Refrain from using abusive language and gestures
  • Refrain from interfering with the coach's practice and game plans by giving contradicting instructions to the team or players while the team is on the court
  • Address questions regarding team activities to the coach. If questions or concerns still have not been addressed satisfactorily, then address with the Community Education Recreation Supervisor, but not with others

Failure to abide by the code of conduct may result in one or more of the following:

  • Individual may be asked to leave the facility
  • The team may be penalized for the individual's behavior
  • Individual may not be permited to attend future Rockwood Community Education activities
