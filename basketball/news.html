Wolves News!

October 27, 2013 - Code of Conduct, Player Agreements and Registration Surveys

With our first practice quickly approaching, it is imperative that ALL players read the Rockwood Player Code of Conduct and submit their Player Agreement. These must both be done before the start of practices on November 4th. Both documents can be found on the Player Expectations page. Additionally, any parents who have not already done so should take a few moments to fill out the registration surveys that I sent out to last year's players as soon as possible. If you are new to the team, or have not had the opportunity to do so yet, you can find the 4th grade survey here and the 7th grade survey here. Thank you to all of you for the opportunity to work with your children this season. I am looking forward to a fantastic year.


October 25, 2013 - Practices and Pre-season Tournament Games

Both 4th grade and 7th grade team calendars have been updated to reflect this years practice schedules, as well as the pre-season tournament game schedule. The pre-season tournament is used to allow coaches to evaluate their standing in the league level that they selected before the season begins in earnest. We will use the outcomes of those games to ensure that our team is placed in the appropriate league to allow for both player growth and an enjoyable experience for everyone. Practice schedules cannot be changed. If you have unavoidable conflicts, please let me know as soon as possible.
