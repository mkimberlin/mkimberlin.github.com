Wolves 1-3-1

Red
1) Point guard passes to the wing and then cuts past a screen by the high post on the strong side.

2) If the cut to the basket by the point guard is unsuccessfull, then then he rolls out the the wing.

The high post seals off his screen and then drops down to post up low.

The low post cuts diagonally up to the elbow to the place that was just vacated by the high post.

The weak side wing fills the spot left by the point guard at the top of the key to reset the offense.

3) A pass to the man at the low post is preferrable, however the high post should also free up at the elbow.
